Author: Satoshi Nakamoto

Publication: 2008

URL: https://bitcoin.org/bitcoin.pdf

Elliptic Curve Digital Signature Algorithm (ECDSA)

Author: ANSI X9.62

Publication: 2005

Description: Technical specification of the digital signature algorithm used in Bitcoin

Lattice Attacks on Digital Signature Schemes

Author: Phong Q. Nguyen, Igor Shparlinski

Publication: Journal of Cryptology, 2006

Description: Exploitation of ECDSA vulnerabilities using lattice attacks
